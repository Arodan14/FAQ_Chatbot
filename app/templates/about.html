About Our FAQ BOT

Welcome to our FAQ BOT. It is built to answer Beykoz University FAQ-style questions quickly and clearly.

The chatbot compares the user's message with a curated FAQ dataset and returns the closest matching answer. It also understands simple greetings and can list the questions currently available in the dataset.

How It Works

  • Instant responses: the chatbot matches the question to the closest FAQ.
  • Focused scope: it is designed for the questions included in the project dataset.
  • Simple interaction: users can ask questions naturally or request the full FAQ list.
